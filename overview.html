CREATE YOUR FCE VENDORS CENTRAL ACCOUNTS

Have your FCE Vendors Central Account with your personal or business email address.

Have the following ready:

    Personal or business address

    Business licenses and/or permits (if applicable)

    Personal and/or Business ID and Tax information

    Bank Account for sales proceeds




Set-Up Your FCE Store and add your products


Once your account has been verified, you can now create your store. Choose from 25 product categories to start adding products. Each product is given a unique Product ID to ensure updated inventory management and store reports.






REACH YOUR CUSTOMERS


Now you are all set to open your store. To attract customers and encourage purchases, FCE has various avenues you can utilize. With AdExpress, improve store and product visibility through on-site and off-site means. You can also get insights for store management, client engagements and more via ExpressU- an online learning platform for Fast China Express Vendors.



GROW YOUR BUSINESS


After that first sale, time to plan ahead and take your store to the next level. FCE has everything you need to scale up and make that brand and dream happen.




BUILD YOUR BRAND EMPIRE

Vendors Guide to Fast China Express Selling


At Fast China Express, we focus on innovation and forward thinking. We provide a new way of highlighting your products and brand. We understand the importance of constantly pushing ourselves to be better at what we do and what we can do. As an Express Vendor, you take pride in delivering quality products and customer service while being true to your core values.

Once you join Fast China Express as a vendor, you will have access to Vendors Central. Your gateway to your FCE brand for store and inventory management, customer service, and payments system to name a few.

FCE offers a wide range of products from 25 categories that you can choose from to set-up your store. FCE uses a unique combination of letters and numbers to identify each individual product for sale on the marketplace.

Engage with customers through product videos where they see its’ functionality and usage. In a changing customer environment, this makes it easy for customers to identify you and your products. Have descriptions, photos, and product features that best describes your product.

And when all is done and that first sale is made, it’s just the beginning of your brand story with Fast China Express. As soon as your store is operational, we optimize your store performance to help you make the next step forward.
